Mega Fail

“After all, to get the whole universe totally wrong in the face of clear evidence for over 75 years merits monumental embarrassment and should induce a modicum of humility.

Science, like all human endeavours, is subject to fads and fallacies.”

Want to get a little depressed and astounded at the same time? Read the rest on futile attempts to explain gravity: http://www.holoscience.com/news.php?article=89xdcmfs

Scientists also have trouble explaining stellar mechanisms:
"Just so. Complexity does, however, provide security of tenure. It allows researchers to waste their talents and our money endlessly playing with computer models to approximate surprising new observations. The work is futile because it is not designed to make predictions whose falsification could end the game. There is no thought of any alternative to the thermonuclear model of stars. It is a self-perpetuating pastime.

Countless billions of dollars have been wasted based on the thermonuclear model of stars. For example, trying to generate electricity from thermonuclear fusion, 'just like the Sun.'"

From: http://www.holoscience.com/news.php?article=ah63dzac
